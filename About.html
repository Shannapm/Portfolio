Story

I've been a (graduated) Photographer since 2017. Since then I've explored different fields of Photography and did some commercial assignments as well as making my own art. I've discovered that what I really love is making art. Then in 2020, because of covid-19 I lost my job at a photo studio. I saw it as a change to explore different work fields and see what else there is. I really enjoy the progress of learning and the endless possibilities of programming.
